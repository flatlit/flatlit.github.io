Godot is a very flexible engine - maybe even too flexible.
It doesn't tell you how to organise your projects.
It doesn't tell you how to arrange your scenes.

But it does provides ways to help structure your project in the best way for you.

In this series we'll look how we use godot and enforce constraints which make developing games easier.

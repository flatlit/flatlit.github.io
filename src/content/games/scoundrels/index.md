## Playing the card game

The best way to learn any card game is to watch others, I recommend these two excellent videos:

- Rulies' [How to Play Scoundrel | The Best 1-Player Card Game of All Time](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Gt2tYzM93h4)
- Riffle Shuffle & Roll's [How to Play Scoundrel: a SOLO DUNGEON CRAWL with a deck of cards!](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7fP-QLtWQZs)

In short though:

- From the deck, you deal 1 card into each room.
- If the card is a black (spades or clubs), then it is a monster
- If the card is diamond is it as weapon (technically it's a shield!)
- If the card is a heart it's a health potion.

I've evolved my beloved grey box into the new Flat Lit Studio logo:

![Flat Lit Studios](../../../assets/logo.png)

It's still flat shaded and lit, and now it's an office studio.

I modelled and textured this in [Blockbench](https://www.blockbench.net/) and it's rendered by [Godot](https://godotengine.org).

I've also created various brand and splash screens as scenes inside Godot for easy reuse.

The majority effort here has been updating all of the social media sites.
Thankfully they all let you drag and drop!
